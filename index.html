Linked requirements

  • F.11

    The satellite shall provide an ADCS system to provide pointing capabilities.

  • F.11.1

    ADCS shall provide primary lunar nadir pointing during the lunar orbit.

  • F.11.2

    ADCS shall provide secondary Sun pointing for power generation.

  • P.11.1

    ADCS shall provide ±1 deg pointing accuracy with LC 99.7%.

  • P.11.2

    ADCS should provide ±0.1 deg pointing accuracy with LC 95%.

  • ADCS_D.13

    ADCS shall provide an external actuator to avoid saturation of internal actuators.
